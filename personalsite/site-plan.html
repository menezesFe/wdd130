Overview

Purpose

If you're looking for a fun way to create Dungeons and Dragons characters, look no further! Charater5e is a website that optimizes a character for any situation you can imagine. Our purpose is to provide Dungeons and Dragons resources for players to access, customize, and buy. These resources will include different character sheets with optimized stats, character builds from literature, and a shop where people can buy dice, miniatures, boards, and more. So whether you are trying to deal the most damage in the least amount of turns, or you are trying to sneak past some pirates to get a treasure unnoticed, character5e will help you optimize that build. Perhaps you would like to track an enemy or gather information instead of sneaking and fighting, our site will help you. If your goal is to simply have fun playing the game, however, our site will recommend fun characters from literature that you can theme your character creation around. And finally, if you are a Dungeon Master looking for themes, maps, and adventures to lead a party through, we offer these resources as well. To new and veteran players, to dungeon masters and players alike, character5e is the place to make your foggy ideas bright. Conquer it all with character5e.

Awesome Characters

Whether you are trying to deal the most damage in the least amount of turns, or you are trying to sneak past some pirates to get a treasure unnoticed, character5e will help you optimize that build. Perhaps you would like to track an enemy or gather information instead of sneaking and fighting, our site will help you. If your goal is to simply have fun playing the game, however, our site will recommend fun characters from literature that you can theme your character creation around. Character creation revolves around six different stats: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. Strength allows a character to hit harder and deal more damage with weapons. Dexterity is how nimble your character is, or how easy it is for it to evade oncoming attacks. Constitution defines how much health points your character has. Intelligence allows your character to cast spells and to know things in game. Wisdom shows how aware your character is of its surroundings. Charisma allows your character to make friends and be sociable. Want to be the best consulting detective around? Check out our Sherlock Holmes build. What about a great warrior like Hercules? Pick up your sword and shield and jump into battle with this build!
